Becomes unpinned (overlay mode), out out flow and casting shadow on content below, when screen size reduces. Becomes pinned back again when screen size is large. Starts of in one of two cases:

1) on large screen is automatically visible and pinned

2) on small screen is hidden, and it remains in overlay (not pinned) mode (with the drag gesture) until screen is large enough to fit it in pinned mode

[panel] and [pinned] are defined

behaviour can be further modified with [auto-hide] and [auto-show] and [pinnable]
